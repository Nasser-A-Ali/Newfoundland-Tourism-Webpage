Gros Morne National Park


Gros Morne National Park can be found on the west coast of Newfoundland and is one spot you won't want to miss if you are visiting the area!


Check out these activities while at Gros Morne:

  • Boating Tours
  • Hiking Trails
  • Camping
  • Theatre and Music

Signal Hill & Cabot Tower


Cabot Tower is a historic landmark on Signal Hill, located in Newfoundland's capital city, St. John's, overseeing the harbor and ocean.

If you're travelling to Newfoundland's East Coast and interested in learning about some of Newfoundland's rich history, Cabot Tower is a must visit and is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the province!
