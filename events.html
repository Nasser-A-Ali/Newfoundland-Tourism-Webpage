Events in St. John's



Newfoundland is an international hub with a diverse selection of creative talent spanning film, theatre and performing arts, visual arts and craft, writing and publishing, and music. Between its rich culture, breathtaking scenery, fully-fledged facilities, delectable cuisines, and vibrant nightlife, there is a wide array of events and activities available. Whether you're into sports, dance, sightseeing, live shows, film, shopping, or unique experiences, you're guaranteed to find something to suit your interests. Here are some authentic Newfoundland events you must experience regardless of the time of year. For more info, please click on the event title.
